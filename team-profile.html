Team Profile

Personality profiles/test outcomes

The results of our three tests show that we all have different personalities, strengths and ways in which we learn, interact and behave with others. We think that this will be advantageous to Team Exodus as different skills and experience will allow us to analyse, plan and work on assessments in a productive and efficient manner. Having different personalities also gives our team the ability to think outside of the box from what we would usually think and do at an individual level.

On a practical sense, the Big Five Personality Test has highlighted traits in which will aid in better collaboration:

  • Practical – scheduling and dividing work (Aileen, Liam, Sergei)
  • Analytical – brainstorming, bigger picture and attention to detail (Maxim, Sergei, Ayub)
  • Empathetic – strong in people skills and keep group on track (Aileen, Xavier, Liam, Maxim, Sergei, Ayub)
  • Logical – understand processes and have a clear coherent path as to direction that something needs to head (Sergei, Aileen, Liam, Xavier)
